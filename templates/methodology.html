Key Assumptions & Risks
HIGH
Synthetic data ≠ real markets. Student-t/GARCH cannot capture event risk (FOMC, earnings), microstructure, or exact VIX-SPX correlation dynamics. Results may overstate consistency.
HIGH
Debit costs are modeled, not observed. Real fills differ by $0.50-2.00/side depending on market conditions, bid-ask spreads, and liquidity at specific strikes.
HIGH
Settlement uses close price, not PM SET. SPX 0DTE settles on the PM settlement value which can differ from 4:00pm, especially on volatile days.
MED
VIX overstatement assumed stable at 1.4×. In reality, the variance risk premium varies: it drops during crises and rises in calm periods.
MED
No commissions/fees modeled. At ~$7.80 per batman (6 legs × $0.65), this is material against $4.34 average daily P&L.
MED
Trap intraday prices are interpolated. Actual SPX path may visit the profit zone at different times than modeled.
Known Weaknesses

Statistical

Circular validation: Backtest runs on data from the same model that informs the strategy. 62% WR may not replicate on real data.
Sharpe overstated: 8.42 is unrealistic. Synthetic data has less variance than real markets, and debit model may underestimate. Realistic estimate: 1.5-3.0.
Limited high-vol data: Only 5 days with VIX >30. Insufficient to validate crisis performance.

Practical

No position management: Backtest assumes static hold to expiry. Real traders adjust, roll, or close early.
Liquidity assumptions: Far OTM 0DTE strikes may have wide bid-asks and require legging in.
No exit/adjustment model: Trap P&L assumes hold to settlement. No mid-day stop-loss or profit-taking rules.
