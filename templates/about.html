About Signal Hawk Capital

Signal Hawk Capital is a research and data-driven technology company focused on Machine Learning (ML) and adaptive rules-based long-term trend investing.

Our proprietary models analyze decades of market data to generate systematic buy and sell signals on the S&P 500 and SPXL. The goal is straightforward: participate in durable uptrends and move to cash when major selloffs begin.

We believe individual investors deserve access to the same quantitative tools used by professional firms — without the complexity or high fees. Signal Hawk distills sophisticated analysis into clear, actionable alerts delivered straight to your inbox or phone.

Our Approach

Signal Hawk's strategy is grounded in price-trend regime detection — identifying when the broad market is in a favorable environment for equity exposure and when it is not. We use a combination of moving average crossover signals, volatility filters, and machine-learning-enhanced regime classification to generate timely buy and sell alerts.

This isn't day trading or market timing in the traditional sense. Our signals are designed for long-term investors who want to stay in the market during sustained uptrends while systematically reducing exposure during major declines — the kind that can set portfolios back by years.
