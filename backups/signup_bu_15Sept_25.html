How Signal Hawk Works

Signal Hawk uses a proprietary, rules‑driven algorithm to help long‑term investors decide when to be offensively positioned in the S&P 500 and when to step aside. The model looks for durable trend shifts and risk conditions (not day‑trading noise) with the goal of participating in major uptrends while avoiding a portion of deep drawdowns.

  • Buy signals are sent when the model detects a confirmed bullish trend. Subscribers typically add S&P 500 exposure, and some choose a tactical vehicle such as SPXL to pursue enhanced upside in strong markets.
  • Sell / reduce‑risk signals are sent when the model detects deteriorating conditions. Subscribers may reduce equity exposure or hold cash/defensive assets until the next buy signal.
  • Alerts are delivered to your email and text the moment a new signal is confirmed.

What is SPXL? Direxion Daily S&P 500 Bull 3X Shares (SPXL) is a daily 3× leveraged ETF designed to deliver approximately three times the S&P 500’s daily return. Because it rebalances every day, returns over longer periods can diverge from 3× due to compounding and volatility effects. Many investors use SPXL tactically during strong uptrends and avoid it when conditions weaken.
