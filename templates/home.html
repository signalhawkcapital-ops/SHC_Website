How It Works

Get Your Strikes

Score the Trap

The trap engine scores today's setup (0–100) using move magnitude, range expansion, and VIX trend. Green = add a center fly. Red = skip. No guessing.

Common Questions
Do you execute trades for me?

No. Wingspan calculates strikes and generates order strings. You copy them into your broker and execute yourself. We are an analysis tool, not a broker or signal service.

What brokers work with the order strings?

ThinkOrSwim (TOS), Schwab, and IBKR. The SPXW format is standard across major options platforms.

How accurate is the backtest?

Debits are modeled using Black-Scholes with VIX overstatement calibration from real data. Results are hypothetical — slippage, execution timing, and commissions are not fully captured. You can run the backtester yourself and see every trade.

What's the minimum account size?

SPX butterfly spreads typically cost $2–6 per side. At 1 contract with 2 sides plus a potential trap, budget $15–25 per day in debit. Most users have accounts of $25K+ for adequate margin and position sizing.
